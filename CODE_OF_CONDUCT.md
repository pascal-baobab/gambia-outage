# Code of Conduct

This project serves everyone in The Gambia — across language, region, religion,
politics and technical skill. The community around it works the same way.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being welcoming to newcomers and non-technical contributors — they are this
  project's lifeblood, not a burden.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the people who depend on the app.
- Showing empathy towards other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, derogatory or discriminatory comments, personal or political
  attacks.
- Publishing others' private information (doxxing) — **in this project, attempting to
  de-anonymize users of the app is treated as the gravest possible violation**, on
  top of being a security incident.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or sexualized language or attention.

## Scope & enforcement

This applies in all project spaces (repository, issues, PRs, Discussions, project
chat groups) and when representing the project publicly.

Report violations privately to **conduct@gambiaoutage.com** or via a direct message
to a maintainer. All reports are reviewed promptly and confidentially. Maintainers
may remove content, and warn, temporarily restrict or permanently ban anyone for
behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening or harmful.

Maintainers who fail to enforce this code in good faith may be removed under
[GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md).
